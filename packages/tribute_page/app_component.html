Thomas Spencer Monson
  • 1927: Born on Aug. 21
  • 1944: Enrolled at University of Utah
  • 1945: Joined United States Naval Reserve
  • 1948: Graduated cum laude with a degree in business management
  • 1948: Married Frances Beverly Johnson
  • 1950: Called as Bishop (age 22)
  • 1955: Called as a counselor in the stake presidency
  • 1959-1962: Served as mission president of the Canadian mission
  • 1963: Called as an Apostle (age 36)
  • 1985: Called to serve in the First Presidency
  • 2008: Called as the 16th President of The Church (age 81)
  • 2018: Died on Jan. 2 (age 90)
